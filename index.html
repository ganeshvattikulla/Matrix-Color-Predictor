  • Starting Balance: You begin with $30.
  • Select a Color: Choose one of the colors to bet on: Red (R), Blue (B), Yellow (Y), or Orange (O).
  • Place Your Bet: Enter your desired betting amount in the "Betting Amount" box and click the "Place Bet" button.
  • Wait for the Next Round: A countdown timer will show how much time is left until the next round starts. Betting is closed during the last 2 seconds of the countdown.
  • Watch the Timer After the countdown, the game will generate a 3x3 grid of symbols.
  • Check for Wins: If there are any complete Column of the color you chose, you win!
  • Winnings: For each matching Column, you earn double your betting amount and based on lines you Selected. Your balance will automatically update after each round.
  • Deposit More Funds: If you want to add more money to your balance, click the "Deposit" button and enter the amount.
  • Play Again: After the results are shown, you can place a new bet for the next round!
  • Have Fun! Enjoy the game and good luck!
